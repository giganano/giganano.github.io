Background

I am primarily interested in the origin of the elements and galaxy formation and evolution. Galactic chemical evolution (GCE) bridges the gap between nuclear physics and astrophysics. By combining galactic processes such as star formation with nucleosynthetic yields from stellar evolution and supernova explosion models, GCE parameterizes the enrichment rates of various elements in the interstellar medium (ISM).
